Frequently Asked Questions

Questions about ordering, materials, and delivery.

Do you offer custom sizes?
Yes — most pieces can be customised. Use the contact form to share dimensions.
What finishes do you use?
We use natural oils, water-based lacquers, and waxes depending on the piece.
Do you ship internationally?
We ship regionally; for international orders we provide a custom quote.
What is the lead time?
Lead times vary by product and season; a typical lead time is 4–8 weeks.
