About Okullo & Sons Furniture

We are a team of designers and craftspeople crafting sustainable furniture with an emphasis on durability and design.

Our Story

Founded by two friends who wanted furniture that lasts, Oak & Anchor blends traditional joinery with modern sensibilities. We source responsibly and work with local suppliers when possible.

Our Process

  1. Design & sketch — We start with a hand-drawn concept.
  2. Material selection — We choose sustainably sourced timbers.
  3. Crafting — Traditional joinery with modern tooling.
  4. Finish & quality control — Natural oils and slow curing.
